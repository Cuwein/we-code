Especially the milky way puff puff

Milky way puff puff: Puff-puff is a popular snack across Africa, especially in the West.
It is essentially fried dough. The name Puff-puff is from Nigeria, many other names and varieties of the pastry exist. The Milky Way Puff-puff is a relatively new variety that has fans on chokehold.
